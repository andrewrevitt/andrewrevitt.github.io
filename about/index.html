About

Andrew Revitt

I started in Information Technology before moving into design.

I developed one of the first mountain bike sites in 1998 and raced events like the Megavalanche, Saas Fee Glacier bike and the Torgonabike from 1997 to 2002.

I've worked for startups and design agencies and have been freelance since 2009.

I've lived in the UK, France and Switzerland and travelled extensively in the alpine regions of Europe.

I'm currently taking on new work - please contact me on the links below.

Design

I’ve created branding, graphic, print and web design for a wide variety of clients. I also developed an online store for design resources and wrote a book on developing Tumblr themes. I am currently developing the Super Alpine brand and magazine.

I enjoy great typography, classic product design and some modern art. Some examples of this would be the Porsche 911, Futura & Helvetica typefaces and the work of Damien Hirst.
